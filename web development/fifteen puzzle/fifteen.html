Fifteen Puzzle

The goal of the fifteen puzzle is to un-jumble its fifteen squares by repeatingly making moves that slide squares into the emopty space. How fast can you solve it?


American puzzle author and mathematician Sam Lloyd is often falsely credited with creating the puzzle; indeed, Lloyd claimed from 1891 until his death in 1911 he invented it.The puzzle was actually created around 1874 by Noyes Palmer Chapman, a postmaster in Canastota, New York.
